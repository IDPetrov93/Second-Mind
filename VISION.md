# VISION

## Why KOS Exists

Modern information is abundant but fragmented.

People do not suffer from a lack of information.

They suffer from a lack of understanding.

News, social media, reports, research papers and official announcements constantly compete for attention, often repeating the same facts with different wording, missing context or conflicting with each other.

Reading everything is impossible.

Ignoring everything is dangerous.

KOS exists to solve this problem.

---

# Mission

KOS continuously builds, maintains and updates structured knowledge about topics that matter to the user.

Its goal is not to collect news.

Its goal is to maintain understanding.

News are only signals that knowledge may need to be updated.

---

# Philosophy

KOS never tries to know everything.

KOS tries to know enough to explain reality.

Understanding is always preferred over information volume.

Context is always preferred over isolated facts.

Evidence is always preferred over opinions.

Traceability is always preferred over confidence.

---

# What KOS Does

For every selected Topic, KOS:

- builds an initial knowledge base;
- identifies trustworthy primary sources;
- continuously monitors relevant information;
- extracts factual Statements;
- validates Claims whenever possible;
- collects supporting and contradicting Evidence;
- enriches the topic with historical context;
- updates the Knowledge Graph;
- synthesizes the current state of knowledge.

Every new piece of information is treated as a possible knowledge update, not as a standalone news story.

---

# What KOS Does Not Do

KOS does not predict the future.

KOS does not give financial advice.

KOS does not make decisions.

KOS does not optimize for engagement.

KOS does not maximize information volume.

KOS does not replace human judgment.

---

# Outputs

Knowledge maintained by KOS may later be transformed into different output formats.

Examples include:

- Daily digest
- Weekly report
- Blog article
- Video script
- Podcast outline
- Research brief
- API response

Outputs are plugins.

Knowledge remains the single source of truth.

---

# Core Principle

The user should never ask:

"What happened today?"

Instead, KOS should answer:

"This is what changed in the topics you care about."

---

# Long-Term Vision

KOS is not a news aggregator.

KOS is not an AI chatbot.

KOS is not a recommendation engine.

KOS is a continuously evolving Knowledge Operating System that helps one person understand the world through structured, verifiable and continuously maintained knowledge.
