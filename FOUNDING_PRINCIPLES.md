# Founding Principles – KOS

These principles are permanent unless explicitly re-evaluated.

---

## 1. Core over Features
We build systems, not features.

---

## 2. Understanding over Content
Content is a byproduct, not the goal.

---

## 3. Knowledge over Generation
Knowledge matter more than output volume.

---

## 4. Simplicity over Complexity
Prefer simple systems that can scale.

---

## 5. Modularity is mandatory
Every component must be replaceable.

---

## 6. No vendor lock-in
No dependency on a single AI model, platform, or provider.

---

## 7. Truth over Speed
Incorrect information has no value.

---

## 8. Transparency over Magic
No black-box behavior without explanation.

---

## 9. Human remains central
System assists thinking, not replaces it.

---

## 10. Everything is reversible unless proven otherwise
No irreversible architectural decisions without strong justification.
